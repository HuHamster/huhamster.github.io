Room People Counter

Learn how to create a simple and effective people counter for any room using an Arduino.

Description

Want to make the same people counter in your room? I'll show you how to do it in 30 seconds. This is an ideal project for beginners that will help you understand how infrared sensors and OLED displays work.

A people counter is an electronic device that uses infrared sensors to detect movement and count the number of people entering or leaving a room. The data is displayed on a small but bright OLED display, making it ideal for real-time occupancy monitoring.

This project is perfect for home use, small offices, or any space where you need to quickly and accurately count the number of people. It's a simple but powerful way to manage a space.
